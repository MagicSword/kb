# Beetroot brownies

## Ingredients

- `80-100g` Nuts (any)
- `600g` Beetroot
- `150g` Protein
- `3 spoons` of Coconut oil
- `200g` of cocoa

## Preparation

1. Mix everything and put it into bowl and stir (ℹ️ use baking paper)
2. Put it into the form and bake it on `180℃` for about `20-30m`
3. Done
